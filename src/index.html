Nick Clawson was born in Los Angeles, but spent his childhood growing up in beautiful Bellingham, Washington. As a kid he pursued a variety of subjects, excelling in everything from filmmaking to 3D modeling and animation. He is currently on his way to earning a BS in Informatics from the University of Washington, with a focus on Human Computer Interaction.

In addition to attending college full time, Nick does web development for the Department of Biology at UW, creating and maintaining services used by thousands of students every quarter. And works with a team to develop an advanced touch screen interface for the UW EcoCar2 team.

When he's not doing extracurriculars, Nick enjoys camping, backpacking, skiing, playing tennis, and attending concerts with his friends.
