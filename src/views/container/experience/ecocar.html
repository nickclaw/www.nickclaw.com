ecocar

I have been a part of the student led EcoCar competition since 2012, working with a team to design and develop a touch screen inferface to replace the center stack of a 2013 Ford Malibu

Taking advantage of version control and weekly meetings, we maintain a very active development cycle. Creating a GUI thats safe, efficient, and attractive.

javascript, html, sass, sencha touch, java

Our touch screen interface earned 2nd place in the second year of the international contest. It is currently being rapidly developed for the final competition.
